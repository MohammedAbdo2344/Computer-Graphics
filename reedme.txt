	Home model

project 12th by mohammed hassan abdo 

this project is about home model in opengl 
user can show home in front with(door,two window) , left side ,
right side and back side with (two window) .
also user can zoom in and zoom out .

To use program use these key :
(1) Left-Key to show left side 
(2) right-key to show right side 
(3) up-key to show right side 
(4) down-key to show right side  
(5) pgup to zoom in 
(6) pgdn to zoom out
